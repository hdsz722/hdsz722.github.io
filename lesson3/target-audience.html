Target Audience

This site is intended for all users interested in the local forcast. The main audience will be for high school and college students plus all adults. The site is setup to be utilized by people on their cell phones and tablets to be able to make a quick check of the weather forcast in their area. It is also designed to be used with larger screened computers to allow for more advanced looks at the upcoming weather forcasts. The site will also provide quick and easy access to weather alerts and advisories.

Personas

Person #1

Adult - age 45

Outdoor enthusiast

Needing to find out the weather conditions for the afternoon before going out fat-biking. Determine what gear will be needed to stay warm and protected from the elements.

Person #2

High School Student - age 17

Boy Scout SPL

Planning a winter campout with the troop. Looking to find out what the weather conditions are looking like for the weekend to make sure they gather the right gear for the temperatures. Hopeing to earn more points toward their 200 below patches on this campout.

Scenarios

  1. What does the weather look like for the next couple of days?
  2. The weather is looking pretty cold, is there anything I need to get prepared for?
  3. I am going to go skiiing this weekend, is there any forcasted new snow on the way?
  4. I am a new driver, I wonder if ther is any icey condictios that i need to be prepared for!
  5. We are going on a vacation to see grandparents, what kind of weather are we going to prepare for on the way?
