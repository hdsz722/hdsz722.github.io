Quarterly DOG Party

This month, the 49ers Packing Iron Dutch Oven group hosted their quartlery Dutch Oven Gathering (DOG) party. 15 members attended this event where they prepared, cooked, and shared their dutch oven skills with each other. Even though the temperature outside was below zero, there was no shortage of fun being had.

As usual, there was a ton of wonderful food at the end of the day. Stews, cassaroles, pasta, and of course jambalaya. We of course can't forget to mention Brian's amazing dutch oven rolls.

To top it all off there were amazing deserts. Cobbler, cake, pie, and my favorite strawberry cheesecake.

These events are always open to everybody that loves cooking in their cast iron or is interested in learning how to cook. We have people with no experience cooking right next to our state champions who have also competed at the IDOS national cookoff in Sandy, UT. Come join us at our next DOG!
