Main Salmon River

Adventure Overview

Our two-day river trip brings you down 40 miles of both scenic floating and exciting rapid navigation. Take your time enjoying the river corridor over the course of three leisurely days, or see it all in just two! The first 20 miles of the trip lend time to warm up to the river trip, with several Class II and II+ rapids. Take in the scenery and wildlife, learn to kayak, or enjoy short hikes for the first half of the trip. The second 20 miles is a fun stretch that features exciting Class III and III+ rapids, the highest concentration of back-to-back whitewater.
