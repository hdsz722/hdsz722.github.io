Welcome to Salmon River Adventures

We’re a professional family of guides dedicated to leading trips down some of the most beautiful rivers in the world. Our most popular trips is the Middle Fork of the Salmon River which is a 7 day trip of you will never forget. We also offer additional shorter trips for the weekend or our 11 day multi-river adventure.

Whether you’re a first time camper or seasoned river veteran, our guides and staff are there to make sure you have the trip of a lifetime. From falling asleep beneath the stars at night to enjoying the refreshing splash of rapids on a hot day, we’ll take care of the details and leave you free to explore. We specialize in group trips, focused on amazing adventures, healthy food, and creating good memories. We will do our best to meet your needs no matter how big or small.

Our staff and guides are excited that you have picked us to join you for your vacation on the river.
